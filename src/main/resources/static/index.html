Payments are processed securely by Stripe and PayPal. You can pay by credit or debit card, as well as Apple Pay, Google Pay, Klarna and AliPay. You'll be able to get a full refund if a package is lost or damaged by the postal service. In case of any issues, feel free to contact me.

The store works worldwide, and shipping is included and FREE for all items. Usually, delivery takes around 1-4 weeks. You'll be able to keep track of your delivery online – just log in with your email.

Pre-orders go to the printing shop first, so please allow 5 additional days for delivery.

Please also note that your country's import taxes are not included in the delivery cost.

Important: Some packages may experience unexpected delays due to strikes by the postal service or the COVID situation.

Original art is sent without a frame in a hand-crafted package with multiple protective layers to ensure safe delivery and your aesthetic pleasure.

Prints are shipped frame-less in branded paper tubes or flat parcels.

You can return original artworks and prints by mail within 7 days of receipt in their original condition and get a full refund, excluding return shipping costs.

We comply with GDPR rules. Your data is protected and stored securely. We do not expose, publish or sell it to any third party. Payments are processed securely by Stripe and PayPal, and the store does not collect your payment data.
